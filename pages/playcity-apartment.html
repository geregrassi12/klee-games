PlayCity - APARTMENT town life

Discover and Explore fun world

Explore this fun building with diverse and friendly characters. Walk the shops, interact with their owners, use and store their objects, feed and serve a drink to your character

Play City - Apartment is a game where you can move around the city recognizing objects and doing the mini activities that each store proposes.

Discover objects and the interactions between them, turn on the lights, turn off the music, take care and water the plants, dry and cut your hair, visit the police station, visit and help the doctor in the hospital, eat sweets, visit the bank, help the firefighters, find magical objects! In Play City - Apartment there are no rules so don't be afraid to explore and interact with everything you find.

The game does not have time countdowns or win and lose situations in order for the child to be able to play freely and comfortably without stress and at the rhythm that he imposes setting up his own adventures.

Features

● Beautiful illustrations

● Fun Animations and Sounds

● Special music for each environment

● Intuitive and child-oriented interface.

● Play City - Apartment is designed for children between 3 and 6 years old, and it can also capture the imagination of much older users since it is designed to explore and be full of surprises.
