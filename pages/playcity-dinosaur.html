Welcome to PLAY WITH DINOS! A new experience where you can immerse yourself in a world full of dinosaurs. Educational game for kids, toddlers or babies.

It is the best dinosaur game for kids from 3 to 6 years old !!

Have fun playing with dinosaurs in PLAY WITH DINOS !. Become a paleontologist and explore his world! Find bones, fossils, use tools and interact with them !! A great educational game for kids, toddlers and babies that we can share with the family !!

It is a dinosaur game designed for kids from 3 to 6 years old, but it can catch the imagination of much older users as it is designed to explore and full of fun surprises! They can develop early stimulation in children with games such as puzzles, memo-tests, mazes or painting their dinosaurs. No annoying advertising while children play for added security and with the possibility of playing without wifi.

Play with Tyrannosaurus Rex, Triceratops, Velociraptors and all kinds of dinosaurs!
