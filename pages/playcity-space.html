Welcome to PLAY IN THE SPACE! A new experience where you can immerse yourself in the space world.

Explore a fun space world. Have fun playing with astronauts in PLAY IN THE SPACE! Become an astronaut and explore the planets! Find planets, constellations, robots, surprises, games, food, use tools and interact with them! A great educational game we can share with the family!

They can develop early stimulation with games such as puzzles, memo-tests, mazes or discovering constellations. No annoying advertising while children play for safety and with the possibility of playing without wifi.

Play on the Moon, Mars, Jupiter and the other planets!

We make our games for the whole family with a lot of love. If you like what we do, don't forget to rate our work!
