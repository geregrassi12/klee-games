About us

Klee Games was conceived with the purpose of developing applications for children to learn and master new skills without neglecting their spirit of the game and fun, and allowing them to experiment through senses and emotions in a healthy, playful, and interactive environment. Our team is made up of professionals highly experienced in the technical, artistic, and pedagogical spheres, thus achieving a synergy that aims at producing quality products.
